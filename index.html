Dama Game Documentation

Welcome to the Dama game! Learn how to play and enjoy.

Objective

The objective of the Dama game is to capture all of your opponent's pieces or block their moves.

Game Rules

  • Each player starts with 12 pieces represented by circles.
  • Players take turns moving their pieces diagonally on the board.
  • You can capture your opponent's piece by jumping over it if the square behind is empty.
  • King pieces can move in any direction, while regular pieces can only move forward.
  • A piece becomes a king when it reaches the opponent's back row.

Game Controls

Use the mouse to click on your piece to select it and then click on a valid square to make a move. The game enforces legal moves and captures.

Winning the Game

You win the game when your opponent can't make any legal moves or when you capture all of their pieces.

Scoring

A message will be displayed when the game ends, indicating the winner: "Black wins" or "White wins."

Responsive Design

The game board adjusts its size to fit the screen. On smaller screens, the board and pieces are scaled down for better gameplay.

Enjoy playing Dama!
